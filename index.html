The AK-47

The AK-47, also known as the Kalashnikov, is one of the most iconic and influential firearms in history. Designed by Mikhail Kalashnikov in 1947, the rifle was built to be a rugged, reliable weapon that could withstand harsh conditions. It became the standard issue for the Soviet military and has since been used by armed forces, guerrilla groups, and civilians worldwide.
